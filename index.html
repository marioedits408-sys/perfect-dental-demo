Patient Information

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

Not at all. With modern local anesthesia and advanced rotary endodontics, a root canal is now as painless as getting a standard filling. Our patients report little to no discomfort during the procedure.

How often should I get my teeth cleaned?

We recommend a professional dental cleaning and check-up every 6 months. This helps remove plaque buildup that regular brushing misses, preventing cavities and gum disease before they start.

Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes, we prioritize dental emergencies such as severe toothaches, knocked-out teeth, or broken crowns. Please call our clinic immediately if you are experiencing an emergency.

Is teeth whitening safe for my enamel?

Professional teeth whitening performed by a dentist is completely safe. We use clinically approved bleaching agents that protect your enamel while lifting deep stains effectively.
